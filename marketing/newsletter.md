## üéì Educational Spotlight

### Understanding Bottleneck Scores

Bottleneck scores range from 0-100, where:
- **80-100 (Red)**: Critical bottleneck requiring immediate attention
- **60-79 (Orange)**: High bottleneck, address soon
- **40-59 (Yellow)**: Medium bottleneck, plan improvements
- **0-39 (Green)**: Low bottleneck, functioning well

**How to use scores:**
1. Identify your highest scores (critical areas)
2. Review corresponding topic details
3. Implement recommended solutions
4. Track score changes over time
5. Measure improvement impact

### Best Practice: Regular Monitoring

**Recommended frequency:**
- **Daily**: During critical periods (regulatory changes, crises)
- **Weekly**: For active monitoring and trend tracking
- **Monthly**: For regular ecosystem health checks
- **Quarterly**: For comprehensive reporting

**Why it matters**: Regular monitoring helps you:
- Identify issues early
- Track trends over time
- Measure intervention effectiveness
- Make data-driven decisions
