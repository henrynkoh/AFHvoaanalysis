## Analytics to Track

- **Reach**: Unique accounts reached
- **Impressions**: Total views
- **Engagement Rate**: (Likes + Comments + Saves + Shares) / Impressions
- **Click-Through Rate**: Profile visits / Impressions
- **Story Views**: For story ads
- **Video Views**: For reel/video ads
- **Saves**: How many saved the post
- **Shares**: How many shared
- **Profile Visits**: From ad to profile
- **Website Clicks**: From bio link
- **Conversions**: Signups, downloads, purchases
